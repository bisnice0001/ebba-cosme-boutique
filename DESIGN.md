# DESIGN DIRECTION & UI/UX SYSTEM: EbbaCosme by Hayyin

This document defines the visual identity and user experience strategy for the **Ebba Cosme by Hayyin** landing page. The goal is to create a digital presence that reflects a "soft feminine luxury" aesthetic while maintaining high conversion potential.

---

# 1. BRAND DIRECTION

### Brand Personality
*   **Sophisticated yet Accessible:** Premium service quality with a welcoming, local touch.
*   **Empowering & Nurturing:** A space where self-care is celebrated.
*   **Meticulous:** Attention to detail in every treatment, from hair to nails.

### Market Positioning
Ebba Cosme by Hayyin positions itself as a **premium community salon** in Karangploso, Malang. It bridges the gap between high-end urban spas and neighborhood salons by offering specialized treatments (like Japanese Spa and Galvanic Spa) at competitive, transparent prices.

### Emotional Tone
*   **Serene:** A digital "breath of fresh air."
*   **Trustworthy:** Professionalism conveyed through clean layouts.
*   **Indulgent:** Visuals that make the user feel they deserve this treatment.

---

# 2. VISUAL STYLE SYSTEM

### Style Direction: Soft Feminine Luxury
We avoid the "tech-heavy" or "minimalist-sterile" look. Instead, we use a **Modern Classic** approach with soft curves, elegant typography, and high-quality imagery.

*   **Whitespace Philosophy:** Generous margins to let the content "breathe." This conveys luxury and prevents the "budget salon" cluttered feel.
*   **Density:** Low to medium. Information is grouped logically into digestible sections.
*   **Photography Direction:**
    *   **Focus:** Close-ups of textures (hair shine, nail art detail, facial glow).
    *   **Lighting:** Warm, natural, and soft.
    *   **Authenticity:** Use real photos of the salon interior (arched mirrors, soft pink walls) to build local trust.

---

---

# 4. TYPOGRAPHY SYSTEM

### Heading Font: **Playfair Display** (or similar Serif)
*   **Reasoning:** Serifs convey tradition, elegance, and luxury.
*   **Usage:** H1, H2, and Section Titles.
*   **Style:** Semi-bold with slightly increased letter spacing for a "fashion magazine" feel.

### Body Font: **Montserrat** or **Inter** (Sans-Serif)
*   **Reasoning:** High legibility on mobile devices and a modern contrast to the serif headings.
*   **Usage:** Paragraphs, UI elements, buttons.
*   **Style:** Regular (400) for body, Medium (500) for navigation.

---

# 5. COMPONENT DESIGN RULES

### Navbar
*   **Style:** Transparent to solid white on scroll. Blur effect (backdrop-filter) for a premium feel.
*   **Links:** Subtle underline on hover using the Primary color.

### Buttons
*   **Primary:** Solid `#8B5C87` with white text. Border-radius: `8px` (not fully rounded, to keep it "sophisticated").
*   **Secondary:** Ghost style with `#8B5C87` border and text.
*   **Interaction:** Slight scale-up (1.02) and shadow increase on hover.

### Cards (Treatments)
*   **Background:** Solid White or `#FAF7F8`.
*   **Shadow:** Very soft, large spread: `0 10px 30px rgba(75, 46, 71, 0.05)`.
*   **Border:** 1px solid `#E7D7E2`.

---

# 6. SECTION-BY-SECTION VISUAL DIRECTION

### Hero Section
*   **Visual:** A split layout. Left side: Bold serif headline and "Book Now" CTA. Right side: A high-quality image of a client enjoying a treatment, framed in an **arch shape** (matching the salon's actual mirrors).

### Service Categories
*   **Layout:** A horizontal scroll or grid of "pills" for categories (Hair, Skin, Body, etc.).
*   **Icons:** Minimalist line-art icons in Deep Mauve.

### Pricing Table
*   **Layout:** Clean list style with "Price from" labels. Use the Sage Leaf (`#A6B07F`) for "Natural" or "Best Seller" badges.

---

# 7. WHAT TO AVOID (ANTI-PATTERNS)

*   **NO** generic "Neon" or "Glow" effects.
*   **NO** harsh black (`#000000`) text; always use the Dark Plum.
*   **NO** stock photos that look too "Western" or "Corporate."
*   **NO** aggressive "Limited Time Offer" countdowns that cheapen the brand.
*   **NO** inconsistent border radii; stick to 8px for most elements.

---

# 8. FINAL VISUAL SUMMARY
The Ebba Cosme website should feel like a **digital extension of the physical salon**. By using the actual interior's arched motifs, the specific "soft feminine" color palette, and high-end serif typography, we transform a simple service list into a **luxury self-care destination**. Every click should feel smooth, and every image should evoke a sense of "I want to be there."
